Music

I began my journey with the French horn when I switched from trumpet in 8th grade.

I received my bachelor's degree in French Horn Perfomance from Brigham Young University, studying with Jon Klein, and continued my studies with John Turman at the University of Washington. There, I received a Master's degree in French horn performance

I'm a freelance musician in the Seattle area. I play as a substitue horn player for various local groups including the Seattle Symphony and Opera. I also teach private horn lessons to students of all ages.
